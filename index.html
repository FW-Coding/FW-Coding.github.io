Projects

Transmission Line Models for Lithium Plating

Developing a novel transmission line architecture to model and detect lithium plating on graphite anodes. Combines EIS data, custom DRT inversion, and AFM measurements to quantify plating severity and validate model predictions. Work conducted in collaboration with the Bazant group and Imperial's ESE group.

TL Models Lithium Plating EIS DRT AFM Python

Parameter Estimation Pipeline

Built a complete parameter estimation workflow from raw cycling data to validated DFN simulations, achieving ~50 mV error. Customised particle swarm optimisation to fit parameters across multiple operating conditions and contributed column mapping for the Novonix cycler to the PyProBE package.

PyBaMM PyProBE PSO DFN Parameter Estimation Python

Electric Race Car Battery Packs

Led a team of five designing and building bespoke battery packs and BMS for Imperial Racing Green's electric race car. Modelled track conditions to determine capacity and current requirements, performed cell selection, pack assembly (spot-welding, soldering), and system integration. Team achieved strong competition performance.

Battery Pack Design BMS Team Leadership Cell Selection Integration

Finite-Volume PyBaMM Implementation

Implemented the first example of PyBaMM applied to a complex fluid-dynamics style system using a finite-volume method in Python. Won a Student Science Communicator award and best poster prize at a PyBaMM/FUSE event for effectively presenting this work to diverse audiences.

PyBaMM Finite Volume Python Fluid Dynamics Science Communication
